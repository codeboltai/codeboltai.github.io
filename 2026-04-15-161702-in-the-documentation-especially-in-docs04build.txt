  (the ctx.* surface ‚ÄĒ skills, capabilities, tool calls, action blocks). Custom 
  Tools & MCP is an authoring surface for building tools that agents, plugins,

  hide a substantial authoring topic (MCP server design, schema, protocol)

  "how your agent uses tools, plus a pointer to the authoring guide" ‚ÄĒ and let
