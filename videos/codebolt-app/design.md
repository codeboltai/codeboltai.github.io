# Codebolt - Design Reference

## Aesthetic Direction

Warm minimalist SaaS UI with editorial calm. The composition should feel human-centered, thoughtful, and analog rather than corporate or neon. Use an off-white canvas, terracotta accents, free-floating editorial groups, restrained motion, and generous whitespace.

Keywords: warm minimalism, editorial calm, human-centered AI, analog intelligence, quiet luxury, thoughtful restraint, anti-corporate softness.

## Typography

- Headings: humanist serif stack such as `Iowan Old Style`, `Source Serif 4`, `Georgia`, serif.
- Body and UI labels: clean sans stack such as `Inter`, `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, Arial, sans-serif.
- Use large, confident type with generous leading and whitespace.
- Completed checklist-style copy may use deliberate strikethrough for storytelling.

## UI Components

- No boxed card grids for video scenes; let type, rules, small marks, and whitespace carry the structure.
- CTAs should feel like floating editorial actions: text, underline, or light accent marks before filled boxes.
- Ghost actions use minimal terracotta strokes only when a control must be interactive.
- Warm-toned rules and small line marks replace panel edges.
- Floating content groups sit directly on the off-white canvas.
- Minimal line iconography, no filled or heavy icons.

## Motion

- Smooth, unhurried transitions.
- Tactile, analog-feeling interactions.
- Loading or processing states should feel calm and intelligent, similar to a "Dimming..." state.

## References

Anthropic, Linear, Notion, and Ink & Switch are the directional references: warmth meets function, low visual noise, and intentional whitespace.
