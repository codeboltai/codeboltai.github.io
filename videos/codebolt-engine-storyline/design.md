# Codebolt Engine Storyline Design

## Direction

Warm minimalist SaaS video with editorial calm and human-centered AI energy. The composition should feel like an intelligent product manifesto, not a boxed dashboard walkthrough.

## Palette

- Cream canvas: `#F5F0EB`
- Soft off-white: `#FAF8F5`
- Warm ink: `#302A25`
- Warm gray: `#5F554D`
- Muted gray: `#8B8178`
- Hairline clay: `#DED2C8`
- Soft clay: `#C8A79A`
- Terracotta accent: `#C96A3A`
- Deep terracotta: `#BD6338`
- Sage completed state: `#798968`
- Dusty lavender tint: `#ECEAF6`
- Warm shadow: `#A75F3F`
- Transparent cream wash: `#F5F0EB`

## Typography

- Headings: `"Iowan Old Style", "New York", Georgia, serif`
- Body/UI: `"Avenir Next", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif`
- Code/data: `"SFMono-Regular", "SF Mono", Consolas, monospace`
- Use large editorial serif display type, generous leading, and quiet sans labels.
- Strikethrough is allowed only for completed loops or resolved iterations.

## Rules

- Do not use boxed cards, framed UI panels, boxed CTA blocks, or nested card layouts.
- Keep content free-floating on the cream canvas.
- Use thin editorial rails, underlines, map routes, constellation lines, dots, and type clusters.
- Terracotta should mark active execution and the core engine.
- Sage should mark completed or retained state.
- Motion should be smooth and unhurried, with tactile cursor-like highlights.
